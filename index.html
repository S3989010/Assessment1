Education

Hobbies

Interest in IT

I like the possibilities and problem solving aspects of IT. For example, during the pandemic, the changes technology has had in terms of day to day life are amazing. I can order almost anything from anywhere delivered to my house. This demonstrates the possibilities that we can create using technology as well as solving a problem for many people who were stuck at home or who were trying to conduct business during the pandemic.

Growing up my older brother was really into computers and gadgets etc. He showed me a lot of what computers could do and how he was able to use that to solve problems that he was facing. I was book keeping for my parents when I was in year 8 at school (1999). My brother helped my create a spreadsheet on the computer to keep track of bills and payments as in those days I had to make phone calls or go into the post office to pay bills and it was hard to track throughout the month.

I am studying online through Open Universities. I am studying Commerce and Majoring in Accounting. When I was choosing my units for study period 4 2022 I was given a list of electives. I chose introduction to IT and Introduction to Programming as they seemed the most relevant to my degree and they happened to be through RMIT.

I hope to gain insight into IT and some relevant concepts that could help me in my chosen career path in the future. I think having some knowledge of how IT works will assist me in problem solving and maybe even give me a leg up in terms of competition relating to employment.

I am currently studying a Bachelor of Commerce majoring in Accounting which would suit this role. I am in the first year still and plan to complete by the middle of 2025. I have predominantly worked within a corporate environment for my whole working life so far where meeting deadlines, being organised and having a strong sense of attention to detail has been imperative. My current role is a management role within a corporate environment and I continue to hone my skill set relating to the above mentioned desired qualifications and experiences.
