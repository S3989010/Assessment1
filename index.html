Project Idea

Overview

My project idea is an app that compares the price of grocery items from stores within the user’s area, to show users where they can find the cheapest options. This allows users to input a shopping list and have all the prices show for each item for each store listed and allows the user to compare the cost and shop for specials.

Motivation

The price of groceries is quite high and there are always deals and discounts available in various supermarkets which are hard to keep track of or to find. I chose this idea to allow people to easily find the items they are looking for and plan their shopping trips accordingly. Using an app to do the work will replace the need for people to look up items individually online or by sorting through catalogues.

Description

My project idea is to create an app that compares the pricing for grocery items in supermarkets and shops within an area that the user chooses. The app will be called Grocery Hunter. I chose this name as it contains descriptive words relating to what the app does as well as provides an easy to find search word when looking for apps related to the word ‘grocery’.

Users will input their postcode to filter the shop results to a particular area. Users can then build a shopping list by entering the items they are interested in purchasing into the app. This will then allow the app to filter results based on the user’s selections of area and items giving them the options of shops available and the price for the items in each of the available shops. The app will take information that is inputted from the user to provide the desired results. The app won’t need user information in the form of a profile or account as users will be prompted to enter in location and items they are searching for each time they use the app.

Outcome

If the app works as its suppose to, users will be able to create shopping lists and plan their trip, to find the best prices for the items they have added. They will also be able to select different locations in case they are planning to travel.
